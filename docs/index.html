Tavolo

See the floor before the next service.

A calmer way to run reservations: guests find a time that fits, and your team always sees who sits where — with a live feel for the room, not a spreadsheet of names.

  • Pick a moment that works for the party, without the back-and-forth.
  • Color tells you in a blink: open, on hold, or in the middle of a meal.
  • One place for the schedule and the table map, from front to back of house.
